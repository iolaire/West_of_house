# Zork I: The Great Underground Empire - Game Overview

## Game Structure

### World Size
- **110 Rooms**: Interconnected locations spanning the surface and underground
- **122 Objects**: Items, containers, treasures, and interactive scenery
- **26 Global Flags**: State tracking for puzzles, NPCs, and world changes

### Maximum Score
- **350 points** achievable through treasure collection and puzzle solving

---

## Major Game Areas

### 1. **Surface World** (Starting Area)
**Rooms**: West/North/South/East of House, Forest areas, Clearing, Path, Stone Barrow

**Key Features**:
- White colonial house (boarded up, must find alternate entry)
- Kitchen window (slightly ajar - can be opened for entry)
- Grating hidden under leaves (entrance to underground)
- Forest maze with climbable tree containing bird's nest
- Stone Barrow (only accessible after winning the game)

**Important Objects**:
- Mailbox with leaflet (tutorial/welcome message)
- Leaves (hide the grating)
- Nest with jewel-encrusted egg (contains clockwork canary)
- Kitchen window (entry point to house)

---

### 2. **The White House Interior**
**Rooms**: Kitchen, Living Room, Attic, Studio, Gallery

**Key Features**:
- Trophy case (where treasures must be placed for points)
- Trap door hidden under rug (entrance to cellar)
- Attic with rope and knife
- Kitchen with food supplies
- Chimney passage (difficult climb requiring specific items)

**Important Objects**:
- Trophy case (treasure repository - 10,000 capacity)
- Rug (hides trap door when not moved)
- Trap door (locked from above, leads to cellar)
- Brass lantern (essential light source, 200 turn battery)
- Elvish sword (glows near enemies, powerful weapon)
- Rope (needed for dome room puzzle)
- Bottle (can hold water)
- Lunch and garlic (food items)
- Painting (treasure)

**Puzzle**: Move rug to reveal trap door, open it to access underground

---

### 3. **The Maze** (15 rooms)
**Rooms**: Maze 1-15, Dead Ends 1-4, Grating Room

**Key Features**:
- Twisty passages, all alike (classic maze navigation)
- Skeleton of previous adventurer with useful items
- One-way passages (diodes) that prevent backtracking
- Grating room connects to surface

**Important Objects**:
- Skeleton keys (unlock grating)
- Rusty knife (cursed - dangerous to use)
- Bag of coins (treasure)
- Burned-out lantern (useless)
- Bones (disturbing them triggers curse)

**Puzzle**: Navigate maze, find skeleton's items, unlock grating

---

### 4. **Troll Room & Passages**
**Rooms**: Troll Room, Cellar, East-West Passage

**Key Features**:
- Troll blocks all exits (must be defeated or pacified)
- Connects cellar to eastern underground areas

**Important Objects**:
- Troll (hostile NPC with bloody axe)
- Bloody axe (weapon, obtainable after defeating troll)

**Puzzle**: Defeat or trick the troll to pass

---

### 5. **Cyclops Area**
**Rooms**: Cyclops Room, Strange Passage, Treasure Room, Maze 15

**Key Features**:
- Cyclops blocks upward passage to treasure room
- Strange passage connects to living room (only when magic flag is set)
- Treasure room contains valuable chalice

**Important Objects**:
- Cyclops (hungry monster, can be fed to pacify)
- Chalice (treasure - silver, intricately engraved)

**Puzzle**: Feed cyclops lunch to make him leave, access treasure room

---

### 6. **Temple Complex**
**Rooms**: North Temple, South Temple, Egypt Room, Torch Room, Dome Room

**Key Features**:
- Religious area with altar and prayer inscriptions
- Dome room with rope puzzle (access to torch room)
- Egyptian tomb with gold coffin

**Important Objects**:
- Bell (brass, can be rung - becomes hot bell when heated)
- Candles (burning, provide light)
- Black book (readable, contains commandment)
- Prayer (ancient inscription)
- Coffin (gold, contains sceptre)
- Sceptre (treasure - Egyptian, sharp point)
- Torch (ivory, flaming - treasure)
- Pedestal (holds torch)
- Altar (container for offerings)

**Puzzle**: Ring bell ceremony to open gates to Land of Living Dead

---

### 7. **Reservoir & Dam Area**
**Rooms**: Reservoir (North/South), Dam Room, Dam Lobby, Dam Base, Maintenance Room, Stream View

**Key Features**:
- Flood Control Dam #3 (controllable water level)
- Reservoir (can be drained by opening dam)
- Maintenance room with control buttons and tools

**Important Objects**:
- Dam (controllable structure)
- Bolt (can be turned to control dam)
- Bubble (green, mysterious)
- Control panel with colored buttons (yellow, brown, red, blue)
- Screwdriver, wrench (tools)
- Tube with putty (Frobozz Magic Gunk)
- Guidebook (tour information)
- Matchbook (light source, one-time use)
- Pump (hand-held air pump for boat)
- Trunk of jewels (treasure, hidden in reservoir mud when drained)

**Puzzle**: Drain reservoir to access hidden areas and treasure

---

### 8. **River & Falls Area**
**Rooms**: Frigid River 1-5, Shore, Sandy Beach, Sandy Cave, Aragain Falls, On Rainbow, End of Rainbow, Canyon Bottom/View, White Cliffs

**Key Features**:
- Frigid River (flows from dam to falls)
- Aragain Falls (spectacular waterfall)
- Rainbow (walkable when visible)
- White Cliffs (narrow passages require deflated boat)
- Canyon (climbable cliffs)

**Important Objects**:
- Inflatable boat (pile of plastic, must be inflated with pump)
- Boat label (instructions for use)
- Buoy (red, contains emerald)
- Emerald (treasure, inside buoy)
- Shovel (for digging in sand)
- Scarab (jeweled, hidden in sand)
- Pot of gold (treasure, at end of rainbow)

**Puzzle**: Inflate boat, navigate river, find rainbow, collect treasures

---

### 9. **Mirror Rooms**
**Rooms**: Mirror Room 1 & 2, Small Cave, Tiny Cave, Cold/Narrow/Winding/Twisting Passages

**Key Features**:
- Two identical mirror rooms (symmetrical layout)
- Enormous mirrors filling entire walls
- Passages connecting to various areas

**Important Objects**:
- Mirror 1 & 2 (enormous, can be broken - bad luck)

**Puzzle**: Navigate symmetrical rooms without breaking mirrors

---

### 10. **Round Room Hub**
**Rooms**: Round Room, Loud Room, Deep Canyon, North-South Passage, East-West Passage, Chasm Room, Engravings Cave, Damp Cave

**Key Features**:
- Central hub connecting many areas
- Loud room (extremely noisy)
- Deep canyon (dangerous drops)
- Engravings cave (ancient inscriptions)

**Important Objects**:
- Platinum bar (treasure, in loud room)
- Engravings (ancient wall carvings, readable)
- Thief (roaming NPC, steals treasures)
- Stiletto (thief's weapon)
- Large bag (thief's loot container)

**Puzzle**: Deal with thief, navigate hub to access all areas

---

### 11. **Atlantis & Underwater**
**Rooms**: Atlantis Room, Reservoir North, Small Cave

**Key Features**:
- Ancient underwater room (accessible when reservoir is drained)
- Connects to mirror room area

**Important Objects**:
- Crystal trident (Poseidon's treasure)

---

### 12. **Hades & Land of the Dead**
**Rooms**: Entrance to Hades, Land of Living Dead

**Key Features**:
- Gateway to afterlife (requires bell ceremony to enter)
- Spirits and ghosts block passage initially

**Important Objects**:
- Ghosts (spirits, block entrance until exorcised)
- Crystal skull (treasure, grinning nastily)
- Bodies (pile of mangled corpses)

**Puzzle**: Perform bell ceremony to gain entrance

---

### 13. **Coal Mine Complex**
**Rooms**: Mine Entrance, Squeaky Room, Bat Room, Shaft Room, Smelly Room, Gas Room, Ladder Top/Bottom, Timber Room, Lower Shaft, Machine Room, Coal Mine 1-4, Slide Room, Dead End 5

**Key Features**:
- Abandoned coal mine with dangerous gas
- Shaft with basket/cage system (raises and lowers)
- Machine room with mysterious equipment
- Slide connects to cellar

**Important Objects**:
- Bat (vampire bat, grabs and relocates player)
- Jade figurine (treasure)
- Raised/Lowered basket (transportation between shaft levels)
- Screwdriver, wrench (tools in maintenance)
- Coal (small pile, burnable)
- Timbers (broken, cluttering passage)
- Machine (large, with switch)
- Machine switch (controls machine)
- Bracelet (sapphire-encrusted treasure in gas room)

**Puzzle**: Navigate mine, avoid bat, use basket system, find treasures

## Key Game Mechanics

### Light System
- Many underground areas are dark (require light source)
- **Brass lantern**: 200 turn battery life
- **Candles**: Burning, provide light
- **Torch**: Flaming, provides light
- **Match**: One-time use, brief light

### Combat System
- **Elvish sword**: Glows near enemies, powerful weapon
- **Knife**: Weapon (nasty-looking)
- **Axe**: Troll's weapon (obtainable)
- **Sceptre**: Sharp point, can be used as weapon

### NPCs & Enemies
1. **Troll** - Blocks passages, has axe, can be defeated
2. **Cyclops** - Hungry, blocks treasure room, can be fed
3. **Thief** - Roams randomly, steals treasures, must be defeated
4. **Bat** - Grabs player and relocates randomly
5. **Ghosts** - Block Hades entrance until ceremony performed

### Container System
- **Trophy case**: 10,000 capacity (treasure repository)
- **Bottle**: 4 capacity (holds water)
- **Coffin**: 35 capacity (contains sceptre)
- **Chalice**: 5 capacity
- **Egg**: 6 capacity (contains canary)
- **Buoy**: 20 capacity (contains emerald)
- **Mailbox**: 10 capacity (contains leaflet)
- **Sandwich bag**: 9 capacity (contains lunch and garlic)
- **Nest**: 20 capacity (contains egg)
- **Raised/Lowered basket**: 50 capacity (transportation)
- **Altar**: 50 capacity
- **Kitchen/Attic tables**: 50/40 capacity
- **Tool chest**: Container for tools
- **Tube**: Contains putty
- **Large bag**: Thief's loot container

## Critical Puzzles & Solutions

### 1. **Entering the House**
- **Problem**: Front door is boarded
- **Solution**: Open kitchen window from outside, enter through window

### 2. **Accessing Underground**
- **Problem**: Trap door hidden, grating hidden
- **Solution**: Move rug to reveal trap door, move leaves to reveal grating, unlock grating with skeleton keys

### 3. **Defeating the Troll**
- **Problem**: Troll blocks passages with axe
- **Solution**: Fight with sword, or give him food (he may eat weapon and die)

### 4. **Feeding the Cyclops**
- **Problem**: Cyclops blocks treasure room
- **Solution**: Give him lunch (hot pepper sandwich)

### 5. **Draining the Reservoir**
- **Problem**: Trunk of jewels underwater
- **Solution**: Turn bolt in dam room to drain reservoir

### 6. **Navigating the River**
- **Problem**: Need to travel on water
- **Solution**: Inflate boat with pump, launch into river

### 7. **Accessing Rainbow**
- **Problem**: Rainbow not always visible
- **Solution**: Specific conditions make rainbow appear at Aragain Falls

### 8. **Entering Land of Living Dead**
- **Problem**: Ghosts block entrance
- **Solution**: Ring bell in temple (bell ceremony)

### 9. **Dome Room Rope Puzzle**
- **Problem**: Can't reach torch room from dome
- **Solution**: Tie rope to railing, climb down

### 10. **Dealing with the Thief**
- **Problem**: Thief steals treasures
- **Solution**: Defeat him in combat, recover stolen items from his bag

### 11. **Narrow Passages**
- **Problem**: Can't fit through with items
- **Solution**: Drop all items (empty_handed flag), pass through, retrieve items

### 12. **White Cliffs Passages**
- **Problem**: Passages too narrow with inflated boat
- **Solution**: Deflate boat to pass through

### 13. **Finding Hidden Treasures**
- **Scarab**: Dig in sandy cave with shovel
- **Pot of gold**: Walk on rainbow to end
- **Trunk**: Drain reservoir
- **Emerald**: Open buoy

## Win Condition

1. Collect all 19 treasures
2. Place all treasures in trophy case
3. Achieve 350 points
4. Stone Barrow opens (won_flag = true)
5. Enter Stone Barrow to complete the game

---

## Game Flags & State Tracking

### Progression Flags
- `won_flag`: Game completed
- `score`: Current points (0-350)
- `moves`: Turn counter

### Area Access Flags
- `troll_flag`: Troll defeated/gone
- `cyclops_flag`: Cyclops pacified
- `lld_flag`: Can enter Land of Living Dead
- `magic_flag`: Magic passage opened
- `rainbow_flag`: Rainbow is walkable
- `low_tide`: Reservoir drained
- `dome_flag`: Rope tied in dome

### Object State Flags
- `rug_moved`: Trap door revealed
- `grate_revealed`: Grating discovered
- `grunlock`: Grating unlocked
- `kitchen_window_flag`: Window opened
- `cage_top`: Basket position
- `deflate`: Boat deflated
- `mirror_mung`: Mirror broken

### NPC Flags
- `thief_here`: Thief present
- `thief_engrossed`: Thief distracted
- `lucky`: Player luck state

### Resource Tracking
- `lamp_battery`: 200 turns of light
- `empty_handed`: No items carried

## Game Design Notes

### Navigation Complexity
- **110 interconnected rooms** create a vast explorable world
- Multiple vertical levels (surface, cellar, mines, shafts)
- Conditional exits based on flags (troll, cyclops, water level, etc.)
- One-way passages and maze sections increase difficulty

### Puzzle Density
- Nearly every room has interactive elements
- Multi-step puzzles require item combinations
- Environmental puzzles (water, light, narrow passages)
- NPC interactions affect world state

### Resource Management
- Limited light (200 turns)
- Inventory capacity constraints
- One-time use items (matches)
- Consumable items (food, water)

### Exploration Rewards
- Hidden treasures require thorough exploration
- Multiple paths to same locations
- Secret passages and alternate routes
- Environmental storytelling through descriptions

---

## Technical Implementation Notes

### Room System
- Each room has unique ID, name, description
- Exits defined as direction → target room mappings
- Conditional exits based on flag states
- Room flags (RLANDBIT, ONBIT, SACREDBIT, etc.)

### Object System
- 122 objects with unique properties
- Type classification (item, container, scenery)
- State tracking (is_open, is_taken, is_lit, etc.)
- Interaction system (verb → condition → response → state_change)
- Size and capacity constraints

### Flag System
- 26 global boolean flags
- Controls room access, object states, NPC behavior
- Persistent across game session
- Affects puzzle solutions and win conditions

## Difficulty Factors
1. **Maze navigation** - Easy to get lost
2. **Limited light** - Time pressure
3. **Thief encounters** - Can lose treasures
4. **Combat system** - RNG elements
5. **Puzzle complexity** - Multi-step solutions
6. **Hidden objects** - Require thorough searching
7. **Conditional access** - Must solve puzzles in correct order
8. **Resource management** - Limited inventory and light
